Frequently Asked Questions

How will my contribution be used?

Your support directly funds project development, software licenses, and the time required to create high-quality content. It helps me stay independent and focused on creating valuable resources for the community.

Is this a one-time or recurring payment?

All contributions through this page are one-time payments. There are no recurring charges or subscriptions involved.

Can I get a refund?

As contributions are considered support for creative work, they are generally non-refundable. However, if you face any issues with the payment process, please contact me, and I'll do my best to assist you.

Is my payment secure?

Yes, all payments are processed through Razorpay, a leading and highly secure payment gateway in India. Your financial information is encrypted and never stored on this website.
