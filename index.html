It’s a simple one-time support. No subscriptions. No hidden charges. Just your gesture of appreciation.

Is my payment secure?

Yes! Payments are securely handled by Razorpay (India’s trusted gateway). Your card or UPI info is encrypted and never stored here.

Can I get a refund if something goes wrong?

Support payments are usually final, but if you face a technical issue or accidental charge, feel free to contact me. I’ll try my best to help.

Will I get anything in return?

Other than my gratitude? 😊 Yes! I may give you early access to projects, updates, or a small thank-you shoutout — if you’d like that.
